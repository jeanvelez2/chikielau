# Product Overview

Chikielau is a dark-themed book review website with a celestial aesthetic. The site features book reviews with star ratings, a live Instagram feed integration, and newsletter signup functionality.

## Core Features

- Book reviews with 5-star rating system
- Live Instagram feed (6 posts by default, configurable up to 25)
- Automatic Instagram token refresh via GitHub Actions (monthly)
- Newsletter signup forms (footer and modal)
- Contact form with email integration
- Bilingual support (English/Spanish)
- Fully responsive design with mobile-first approach
- Animated starfield background

## Target Audience

Book lovers interested in contemporary fiction, fantasy, historical fiction, and science fiction reviews presented in a dramatic, celestial-themed aesthetic.

## Brand Identity

- Dark celestial theme with gold accents
- Dramatic, sophisticated aesthetic
- Typography: Playfair Display (headings) and Lato (body)
- Color palette: Deep blacks (#1A1A1A, #2C2C2C) with gold highlights (#D4AF37) and cream text (#FFF8E7)
