The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

by V.E. Schwab

65% complete

This book is absolutely captivating! The concept of a woman who makes a Faustian bargain to live forever but be forgotten by everyone she meets is both heartbreaking and beautiful. Schwab's prose is lyrical and the way she weaves together past and present is masterful. I'm completely absorbed in Addie's journey through the centuries.
