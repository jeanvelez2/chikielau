Review: The Midnight Library

January 20, 2026

Matt Haig explores the infinite possibilities of life through a magical library between life and death. A thought-provoking meditation on regret, choice, and what makes a life worth living. This book will challenge your perspective on the paths not taken and remind you that every life has value, even when it doesn't feel that way.
