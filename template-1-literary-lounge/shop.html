Book Recommendations

Discover my carefully curated collection of must-read books. Each recommendation comes from the heart and includes affiliate links to support this bookish corner of the internet. Happy reading!

The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

A dazzling novel about all the choices that go into a life well lived. Between life and death there is a library, and within that library, the shelves go on forever. A thought-provoking exploration of regret, possibility, and what makes life worth living.

Buy Now

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

by Taylor Jenkins Reid

A mesmerizing journey through the splendor of old Hollywood into the harsh realities of the present day. A legendary actress reveals her darkest secrets in this unforgettable story about love, identity, and the price of fame.

Buy Now

The Song of Achilles

by Madeline Miller

An achingly beautiful retelling of the Trojan War and the love story at its center. A tale of gods, kings, immortal fame and the human heart. Miller's prose is lyrical and the characters are rendered with stunning depth.

Buy Now

Circe

by Madeline Miller

A bold and subversive retelling that gives voice to one of mythology's most misunderstood characters. The story of the sorceress who transforms Odysseus's crew into pigs. A masterclass in character development and feminist storytelling.

Buy Now

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

by V.E. Schwab

A woman who makes a Faustian bargain to live forever but be forgotten by everyone she meets. Schwab's prose is lyrical and the way she weaves together past and present is masterful. An unforgettable story about art, love, and legacy.

Buy Now

The Night Circus

by Erin Morgenstern

A mesmerizing tale of two young magicians bound in a deadly competition. Set within a mysterious circus that arrives without warning and is only open at night. Atmospheric, enchanting, and utterly captivating from start to finish.

Buy Now

The Starless Sea

by Erin Morgenstern

A love letter to stories and storytelling itself. A graduate student discovers a mysterious book that leads him to an ancient underground library. Intricate, magical, and beautifully written. Perfect for lovers of literary fantasy.

Buy Now

Mexican Gothic

by Silvia Moreno-Garcia

A reimagining of the gothic suspense novel set in 1950s Mexico. A socialite travels to a remote mansion to check on her cousin and encounters terrors beyond imagination. Atmospheric, creepy, and utterly unputdownable.

Buy Now

The House in the Cerulean Sea

by TJ Klune

A heartwarming fantasy about found family and acceptance. A caseworker is sent to investigate an orphanage housing magical children. Charming, uplifting, and full of hope. The perfect comfort read for any season.

Buy Now

Piranesi

by Susanna Clarke

A mind-bending mystery set in an infinite house of endless halls and corridors. Piranesi lives alone, tending to the House and its mysteries. Strange, beautiful, and utterly unique. A meditation on memory, identity, and wonder.

Buy Now

The Priory of the Orange Tree

by Samantha Shannon

An epic standalone fantasy featuring dragons, queens, and ancient magic. A world divided by a thousand-year-old schism faces the return of an ancient enemy. Sweeping, ambitious, and beautifully crafted. Perfect for epic fantasy lovers.

Buy Now

The Atlas Six

by Olivie Blake

Six magicians are recruited to compete for a place in the Alexandrian Society, the world's most prestigious magical organization. Dark academia meets morally gray characters in this addictive fantasy. Clever, complex, and utterly compelling.

Buy Now
