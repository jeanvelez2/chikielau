Project Hail Mary: Science Meets Heart

December 28, 2025 by Chikielau

Project Hail Mary

by Andy Weir

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

Genre: Science Fiction

Published: 2021

Pages: 496

The Verdict

Project Hail Mary — Andy Weir's latest is a thrilling space adventure with humor and heart. Perfect for fans of The Martian who want something even more ambitious.

What It's About

Ryland Grace wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory of how he got there. He's the sole survivor of a desperate mission to save Earth from an extinction-level threat.

What I Loved

The science is fascinating without being overwhelming, and the friendship that develops is one of the most heartwarming relationships in recent sci-fi. Weir's humor shines throughout.

Who Should Read This

Fans of hard sci-fi with heart, problem-solving narratives, and unlikely friendships across the cosmos.

“I penetrated the outer hull of an alien vessel with a homemade drill. So I got that going for me.”

— Andy Weir, Project Hail Mary
