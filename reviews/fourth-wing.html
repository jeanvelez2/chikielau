Fourth Wing: Dragons, Romance, and Deadly Academia

January 10, 2026 by Chikielau

Fourth Wing

by Rebecca Yarros

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

Genre: Fantasy, Romance

Published: 2023

Pages: 528

The Verdict

Fourth Wing — Rebecca Yarros delivers an addictive blend of dragon riders, forbidden romance, and deadly academia that will keep you turning pages late into the night.

What It's About

Violet Sorrengail was supposed to enter the Scribe Quadrant, but her commanding mother forces her into the Riders Quadrant instead, where the weak die and the strong bond with dragons.

What I Loved

The world-building is immersive, the romance is swoon-worthy, and the stakes feel genuinely high. Yarros balances action, politics, and character development masterfully.

Who Should Read This

If you love dragon fantasy, enemies-to-lovers romance, or military academy settings, this is your next obsession.

“A dragon without its rider is a tragedy. A rider without its dragon is dead.”

— Rebecca Yarros, Fourth Wing
