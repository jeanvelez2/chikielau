A Court of Thorns and Roses: Beauty and the Beast Reimagined

December 1, 2025 by Chikielau

A Court of Thorns and Roses

by Sarah J. Maas

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ Rating: 4 out of 5 stars

Genre: Fantasy, Romance

Published: 2015

Pages: 432

The Verdict

A Court of Thorns and Roses — A Beauty and the Beast retelling with fae politics, romance, and adventure. The series that launched a thousand BookTok videos.

What It's About

Feyre is a mortal huntress dragged into the magical land of Prythian after killing a fae wolf. Held captive by the High Lord Tamlin, she discovers a world of beauty and danger.

What I Loved

The fairy tale retelling framework gives way to something much more complex and surprising. The world of Prythian is richly imagined, and the romance builds beautifully.

Who Should Read This

Fans of fairy tale retellings, fae romance, or anyone looking for a series that gets better with each book.

“Be glad of your human heart, Feyre. Pity those who don't feel anything at all.”

— Sarah J. Maas, A Court of Thorns and Roses
