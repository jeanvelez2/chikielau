The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo: A Tale of Old Hollywood Glamour

January 5, 2026 by Chikielau

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

by Taylor Jenkins Reid

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Rating: 5 out of 5 stars

Genre: Historical Fiction, Romance

Published: 2017

Pages: 400

The Verdict

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo — A captivating tale of Old Hollywood glamour, secrets, and the price of fame. Taylor Jenkins Reid crafts a story that's impossible to put down.

What It's About

Aging Hollywood icon Evelyn Hugo finally tells the story of her glamorous and scandalous life, including the truth about her seven husbands and the love of her life.

What I Loved

Evelyn is one of the most compelling characters I've ever read. She's flawed, ambitious, and utterly fascinating. The way Reid weaves together past and present is masterful.

Who Should Read This

Anyone who loves stories about complex women, Old Hollywood, or the price of ambition. This book will surprise you.

“I think we all have the potential to fall in love a thousand times in our lifetime. It's easy. The first time you fall, it's the hardest.”

— Taylor Jenkins Reid, The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo
