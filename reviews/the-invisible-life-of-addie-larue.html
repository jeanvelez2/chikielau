The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue: A Story of Memory and Legacy

December 8, 2025 by Chikielau

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

by V.E. Schwab

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

Genre: Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Published: 2020

Pages: 448

The Verdict

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue — V.E. Schwab weaves a haunting tale of a woman cursed to be forgotten by everyone she meets, spanning 300 years of art, love, and defiance.

What It's About

In 1714, Addie LaRue makes a Faustian bargain to live forever but is cursed to be forgotten by everyone she meets. Three centuries later, a young man in a bookshop remembers her name.

What I Loved

The dual timeline is beautifully handled, and Addie's resilience across centuries is inspiring. Schwab's prose is lyrical and the exploration of what it means to leave a mark on the world is profound.

Who Should Read This

Lovers of atmospheric fantasy, stories about art and legacy, and anyone who's ever felt invisible.

“What is a person, if not the marks they leave behind?”

— V.E. Schwab, The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue
