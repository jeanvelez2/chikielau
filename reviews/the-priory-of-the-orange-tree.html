The Priory of the Orange Tree: An Epic Standalone Fantasy

November 10, 2025 by Chikielau

The Priory of the Orange Tree

by Samantha Shannon

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ Rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

Genre: Epic Fantasy

Published: 2019

Pages: 848

The Verdict

The Priory of the Orange Tree — Samantha Shannon delivers an epic standalone fantasy with dragons, queens, and a world on the brink of destruction. No series commitment required.

What It's About

In a world divided by religion and politics, an ancient evil stirs. Queen Sabran must conceive an heir to maintain a magical protection, while across the world, a dragonrider and a mage hold keys to the truth.

What I Loved

The scope is breathtaking for a standalone. Shannon weaves together multiple POVs across different cultures with skill. The representation is excellent and the dragon lore is fresh.

Who Should Read This

Fans of epic fantasy who want a complete story in one book. Think Game of Thrones meets feminist fantasy.

“The mind is its own place, and in itself can make a heaven of hell, a hell of heaven.”

— Samantha Shannon, The Priory of the Orange Tree
